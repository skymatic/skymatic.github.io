Defendor – The High Security Solution for Your Company

Defendor is the central data node in your company and protects your company data securely against ransomware, viruses, data theft, unauthorized access and data loss through automatic backups.

Defendor is installed on your enterprise server and provides network drives for every workstation in your organization. A clear dashboard gives you a comprehensive overview of file accesses and possible threats at all times.

Cryptomator – The Vault for Your Cloud

Cryptomator adds end-to-end encryption to your Dropbox, Google Drive, or any other cloud storage service. This means that virtually any cloud storage can be used securely. If a file should ever fall into the hands of an unauthorized third party, it is still securely protected due to encryption. Since transparency and security go hand in hand, Cryptomator is open-source software. Cryptomator is available for Windows, macOS, Linux, iOS, and Android.
