IT Security Basic Check

Especially in small and medium-sized businesses, infrastructure and sensitive files are often not sufficiently protected against unauthorized access and data loss. Things are supposed to be different with you: As part of the IT security basic check, we analyze your system landscape and advise you on the technical security of your critical data.

The result of the basic IT security check is a prioritized list of measures that you can use to take effective steps to improve your security level, often with little time and cost.

On request, we can also train your employees, raise their awareness of IT security and thus specifically prevent the appearance of “shadow IT”, i.e. the use of software and services that should not be used in your company.

The exact content of the basic check depends on your IT environment and is tailored to your company's needs.
